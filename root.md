# The Virtual Brain (TVB) Wiki

Welcome to the TVB knowledge base. This wiki documents concepts, methods, and software related to whole-brain modeling using The Virtual Brain (TVB) platform.

## What is TVB?

[[TVB]] is an open-source platform for constructing and simulating personalized brain network models. It integrates individual structural data ([[structural-connectivity]] from [[neuroimaging-dti]]) with large-scale brain dynamics simulations to bridge multiple scales of brain organization.
