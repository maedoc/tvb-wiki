# Wilson-Cowan model of the excitatory and inhibitory population dynamics

**Authors**: Alain Destexhe, Terrence J. Sejnowski  
**Published**: 2009  
**Journal**: Scholarpedia  
**DOI**: 10.4249/scholarpedia.1389

## Summary

Authoritative review of the Wilson-Cowan model, written by two leading computational neuroscientists. Provides historical context, mathematical details, and modern extensions. Scholarpedia entries are peer-reviewed and serve as definitive references.

## Key Contributions

- Comprehensive mathematical formulation of the original 1972/1973 models
- Historical context and relationship to earlier work (Beurle 1956, Griffith 1963)
- Modern extensions and applications
- Parameter ranges for realistic dynamics

## Content Structure

- Original derivation from probabilistic neuron dynamics
- Phase plane analysis and bifurcation structure
- Spatial extensions and neural field theory
- Applications to cortical oscillations and disease states
