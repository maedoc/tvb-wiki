## Summary
The seminal textbook on nonlinear dynamics and chaos theory, providing accessible introduction to bifurcation theory, phase portraits, limit cycles, and strange attractors. Widely used in computational neuroscience for understanding neural mass model dynamics.

## Key Concepts
- Phase plane analysis and nullclines
- Bifurcation types (saddle-node, transcritical, pitchfork, Hopf)
- Limit cycles and Poincaré-Bendixson theorem
- Chaos and the Lorenz system
- Applications to biological oscillators

## Relevance to TVB
- Foundation for understanding [[neural mass model]] bifurcations
- Used to analyze [[epilepsy modeling]] seizure transitions
- Underlies [[bifurcation analysis]] in whole-brain simulations
