# The UK Biobank Imaging Study: 100,000 Participants and Beyond

**Authors:** Tim J. Littlejohns, Karlheinz Holliday, et al.  
**Year:** 2020  
**Venue:** Nature Communications

## Key Contributions

- Overview of UK Biobank Imaging Study at 100,000 participants
- Description of imaging protocol and data release
- Summary of early scientific findings
- Future directions for the study

## Abstract Summary

This paper provides an overview of the UK Biobank Imaging Study as it reaches its initial target of 100,000 participants. The authors summarize the imaging protocol, data release schedule, and early scientific findings that demonstrate the value of this resource.

## Significance

Documents the achievement of the 100,000 participant milestone and the scientific impact of the UK Biobank Imaging Study. Provides a roadmap for future extensions of the study.
