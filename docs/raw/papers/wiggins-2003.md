## Summary
Graduate-level text on nonlinear dynamics with emphasis on geometrical methods and applications. Covers invariant manifolds, normal forms, bifurcations, and chaotic dynamics with physical examples.

## Key Concepts
- Invariant manifolds and foliations
- Normal form transformations
- Hamiltonian and dissipative systems
- Chaotic transport and mixing
- Perturbation methods

## Relevance to TVB
- Geometric methods for [[neural mass model]] analysis
- Normal forms for [[bifurcation analysis]]
- Foundation for understanding [[brain oscillations]]
