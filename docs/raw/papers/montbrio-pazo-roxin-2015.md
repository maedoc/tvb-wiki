## Summary
Rigorous derivation of low-dimensional mean field equations from networks of quadratic integrate-and-fire neurons. Provides exact macroscopic description of spiking network dynamics.

## Key Concepts
- Quadratic integrate-and-fire neurons
- Ott-Antonsen ansatz
- Exact mean field reduction
- Macroscopic neural mass equations
- Finite-size effects

## Relevance to TVB
