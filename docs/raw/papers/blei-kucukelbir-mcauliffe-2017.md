## Summary
Accessible review of variational inference for statisticians. Covers mean-field VI, stochastic VI, and modern developments including black-box variational inference.

## Key Concepts
- Variational inference fundamentals
- Mean-field and structured approximations
- Stochastic variational inference
- Black-box variational inference
- Comparison to MCMC

- Scalable inference for large models
- Complements [[dynamic causal modeling]] methods
