# Large-scale recording of neuronal ensembles

**Authors:** Gyorgy Buzsáki, Costas A Anastassiou, Christof Koch  
**Year:** 2012  
**Venue:** Nature Neuroscience 15(3):407-409

## Summary
Review of extracellular recording methods and the relationship between local field potentials, multi-unit activity, and EEG/MEG. Discusses how different recording scales capture different aspects of neural activity.

## Key Contributions
- Relationship between LFP, MUA, and EEG/MEG
- Volume conduction and field potential spread
- Multi-scale recording considerations
- Interpretation of macroscopic signals

## Significance for Whole-Brain Modeling
Clarifies how neural mass models (which often output LFP-like signals) relate to empirical EEG/MEG. Important for forward modeling from simulations to predicted recordings.
