## Summary
Foundational paper on variational Bayesian inference for neuroimaging, introducing the Laplace approximation for model inversion. Forms the basis for Dynamic Causal Modeling (DCM) parameter estimation.

## Key Concepts
- Variational Bayes and free energy
- Laplace approximation for posteriors
- Model evidence and complexity
- EM algorithm for nonlinear systems
- Prior and posterior densities

## Relevance to TVB
- Statistical foundation for [[dynamic causal modeling]]
- Parameter estimation in [[neural mass model]] fitting
- [[variational bayes]] for model inversion
