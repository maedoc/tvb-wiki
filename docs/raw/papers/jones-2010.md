# Challenges and limitations of quantifying brain connectivity in vivo with diffusion MRI

**Author:** Derek K Jones  
**Year:** 2010  
**Venue:** NeuroImage 73:184-193

## Summary
Comprehensive review of diffusion MRI methods for connectivity mapping, including technical challenges, validation issues, and limitations of tractography for inferring connectivity.

## Key Contributions
- Critical evaluation of tractography methods
- Validation challenges for structural connectivity
- Distance and crossing fiber limitations
- Recommendations for best practices

## Significance for Whole-Brain Modeling
Important caveats for interpreting structural connectivity data used in models. Understanding limitations helps assess uncertainty in model predictions derived from DTI data.
