# Functional Connectomics from Resting-State fMRI

**Authors:** Stephen M. Smith, Diego Vidaurre, et al.  
**Year:** 2013  
**Venue:** Trends in Cognitive Sciences

## Key Contributions

- Review of functional connectomics methods
- Discussion of network estimation from resting-state data
- Treatment of temporal dynamics in functional connectivity
- Future directions for the field

## Abstract Summary

This review discusses methods and findings in functional connectomics - the study of functional brain networks. The authors cover network estimation, analysis methods, and the interpretation of functional connectivity patterns.

## Significance

A key review article defining the field of functional connectomics. Provides a framework for understanding resting-state functional connectivity as a window into brain organization.
