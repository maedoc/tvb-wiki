## Summary
The definitive graduate-level text on nonlinear dynamics and bifurcation theory. Covers local and global bifurcations, center manifold theory, normal forms, and applications to physical and biological systems.

## Key Concepts
- Center manifold theorem and reduction
- Normal form theory
- Local bifurcations (codimension 1 and 2)
- Global bifurcations and homoclinic orbits
- Melnikov's method

## Relevance to TVB
- Mathematical foundation for [[bifurcation analysis]]
- Used in analyzing [[wilson-cowan]] and [[jansen-rit]] model dynamics
- Essential for [[epilepsy modeling]] seizure onset mechanisms
