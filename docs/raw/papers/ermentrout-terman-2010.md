## Summary
Comprehensive mathematical treatment of computational neuroscience. Covers single neuron dynamics, neural networks, and continuum models with rigorous mathematical foundations and clear biological motivation.

## Key Concepts
- Hodgkin-Huxley and reduced neuron models
- Firing rate models and mean field theory
- Traveling waves and pattern formation
- Stochastic differential equations in neuroscience
- Phase reduction and coupled oscillators

## Relevance to TVB
- Direct foundation for [[neural mass model]] mathematics
- [[mean field theory]] for population dynamics
- Links [[spiking neural networks]] to mass models
