# The Basics of Brain Development

**Authors:** Joan Stiles, Terry L. Jernigan  
**Year:** 2010  
**Venue:** Neuropsychology Review

## Key Contributions

- Comprehensive overview of neurodevelopment from prenatal to postnatal periods
- Integration of genetic and environmental influences on brain development
- Discussion of sensitive periods and developmental plasticity
- Review of structural and functional developmental milestones

## Abstract Summary

This review provides a comprehensive overview of human brain development, covering neurogenesis, migration, differentiation, synaptogenesis, pruning, and myelination. The authors discuss how genetic programs and environmental factors interact to shape brain structure and function across development.

## Significance

Foundational reference for understanding the biological mechanisms underlying brain development, essential for computational models that incorporate developmental processes.
