## Summary
Seminal review proposing the free energy principle as a unifying framework for brain function. Argues that brain minimizes surprise through prediction and action, connecting perception, inference, and behavior.

## Key Concepts
- Free energy minimization
- Prediction error and surprise
- Active inference
- Bayesian brain hypothesis
- Self-organizing systems
