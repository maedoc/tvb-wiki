# Robust determination of the fibre orientation distribution in diffusion MRI

**Authors:** Jacques-Donald Tournier, Fernando Calamante, David G Gadian, Alan Connelly  
**Year:** 2007  
**Venue:** NeuroImage 35(4):1459-1472

## Summary
Introduced constrained spherical deconvolution (CSD) for resolving multiple fiber orientations within single voxels. Overcame limitations of DTI in regions with crossing fibers.

## Key Contributions
- Constrained spherical deconvolution (CSD)
- Resolution of crossing fibers
- Improved tractography in complex regions
- FOD (fibre orientation distribution) estimation

## Significance for Whole-Brain Modeling
Better structural connectivity estimates in crossing-fiber regions lead to more accurate network models. CSD is now standard in high-quality connectome reconstruction.
