## Definition
Nonlinear dynamics studies systems where output is not proportional to input, giving rise to complex behaviors including oscillations, multistability, chaos, and pattern formation that cannot occur in linear systems.

## Characteristics of Nonlinear Systems

### Superposition Failure
- Response to combined inputs ≠ sum of individual responses
- Interactions between components are essential
- Cannot decompose into independent modes

### Rich Phenomenology
- Multiple stable states (multistability)
- Self-sustained oscillations
- Chaos and sensitive dependence
- Pattern formation and waves

### Nonlinear Phenomena
- Saturation and threshold effects
- Hysteresis
- Frequency entrainment
- Mode locking
