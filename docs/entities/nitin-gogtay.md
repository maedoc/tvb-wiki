# Nitin Gogtay

Developmental neuroscientist known for longitudinal neuroimaging studies of brain development.

## Key Contributions

- Led landmark longitudinal MRI study tracking cortical development from childhood to adulthood
- Established "back-to-front" pattern of cortical maturation
- Demonstrated hierarchical maturation of association cortices

## Major Work

### Dynamic Mapping of Human Cortical Development (2004)
- Longitudinal study of 13 children scanned repeatedly from ages 4-21
- Showed sensory/motor regions mature first, followed by temporal/parietal, then prefrontal cortex
- Established spatial-temporal pattern of human brain development

## Research Focus
- Developmental trajectories of brain structure
- Childhood-onset schizophrenia
- Neurodevelopmental disorders
