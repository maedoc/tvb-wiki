# Rolf Kötter

Neuroscientist, co-originator of the connectome concept, known for work on neuroanatomy and connectivity analysis.

## Key Contributions

- **Connectome Concept** (2005): Co-authored the seminal paper with Sporns and Tononi
- **Neuroanatomical Analysis**: Contributed methods for analyzing brain connectivity patterns
- **Connectivity Databases**: Worked on systems for organizing neuroanatomical connectivity data

## Research Focus

Kötter's research has focused on:
- Neuroanatomical connectivity
- Computational neuroanatomy
- Brain connectivity databases and analysis tools
