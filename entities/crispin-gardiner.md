## Overview
Crispin W. Gardiner is a New Zealand physicist and Professor at the University of Otago. He is renowned for his contributions to quantum optics and stochastic methods, particularly the "Handbook of Stochastic Methods" which is a standard reference worldwide.

## Key Contributions
- **Stochastic Methods**: Author of "Stochastic Methods: A Handbook for the Natural and Social Sciences"
- **Quantum Optics**: Development of quantum stochastic methods and master equations
- **Fokker-Planck Methods**: Techniques for solving Fokker-Planck equations
- **Simulation Methods**: Stochastic simulation algorithms and approximations
