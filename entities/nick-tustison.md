# Nick Tustison

Developer of N4ITK and DiReCT algorithms in ANTs, expert in MRI preprocessing.

## Research Focus

- MRI bias field correction (N4ITK)
- Cortical thickness measurement (DiReCT)
- ANTs toolkit development
- Neuroimaging preprocessing pipelines

## Key Publications

- Tustison et al. (2010) — N4ITK bias correction tustison-2010
  - Improved N3 algorithm with B-spline fitting
  - Faster convergence and noise robustness
  - De facto standard preprocessing step

- Tustison et al. (2014) — ANTs cortical thickness tustison-2014
  - Large-scale comparison with FreeSurfer
  - Test-retest reliability evaluation
  - Competitive or superior ANTs performance
