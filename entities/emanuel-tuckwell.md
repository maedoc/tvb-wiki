## Overview
Henry C. Tuckwell is a mathematician and theoretical neuroscientist known for his foundational work on stochastic processes in neuroscience. His two-volume work on theoretical neurobiology established mathematical frameworks for understanding neural computation.

## Key Contributions
- **Theoretical Neurobiology**: Author of the comprehensive two-volume "Introduction to Theoretical Neurobiology"
- **Stochastic Methods**: Mathematical analysis of stochastic processes in neurons
- **Cable Theory**: Stochastic extensions to cable theory for dendritic computation
- **Population Dynamics**: Mathematical models of neural population activity
