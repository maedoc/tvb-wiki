## Summary
Review of metastability and dynamical regimes in resting-state brain activity. Proposes that brain operates at criticality near bifurcations, explaining rich spatiotemporal patterns and their relationship to cognitive function.

## Key Concepts
- Metastability and dynamical regimes
- Criticality and bifurcations in brain dynamics
- Resting-state networks as attractor states
- Structure-function relationship
- Whole-brain computational modeling
