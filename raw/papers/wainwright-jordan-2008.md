## Summary
Comprehensive monograph on graphical models and variational inference. Covers exponential families, message passing, and variational methods with connections to statistical physics.

## Key Concepts
- Exponential family distributions
- Graphical model structure
- Variational message passing
- Bethe approximation
- Mean field and structured approximations

## Relevance to TVB
- Graphical model foundation for [[dynamic causal modeling]]
- Message passing for [[effective connectivity]]
- Statistical framework for network inference
