## Summary
Original paper introducing Dynamic Causal Modeling (DCM) for inferring effective connectivity from neuroimaging data. Combines biologically motivated neural mass models with Bayesian inference.

## Key Concepts
- Effective connectivity definition
- Neural mass model state equations
- Bayesian model inversion
- Model comparison and selection
- Endogenous and modulatory connectivity

## Relevance to TVB
- Foundation of [[dynamic causal modeling]]
- Links to [[neural mass model]] formulation
- Method for [[effective connectivity]] estimation
