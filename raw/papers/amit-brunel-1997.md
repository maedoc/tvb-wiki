## Summary
Seminal paper on balanced networks showing how cortical circuits maintain low-rate asynchronous activity through excitation-inhibition balance. Foundation for understanding cortical spontaneous activity.

## Key Concepts
- Balanced excitation and inhibition
- Asynchronous irregular activity
- Sparse connectivity effects
- Mean field analysis
- Low-rate persistent activity
