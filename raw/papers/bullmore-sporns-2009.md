# Complex Brain Networks: Graph Theoretical Analysis of Structural and Functional Systems

**Authors:** Edward T. Bullmore, Olaf Sporns  
**Year:** 2009  
**Venue:** Nature Reviews Neuroscience

## Key Contributions

- Comprehensive review of graph theory applications in neuroscience
- Integration of structural and functional connectivity analysis
- Discussion of small-world, scale-free, and modular network properties
- Clinical applications of network analysis

## Abstract Summary

This review article discusses the application of graph theoretical analysis to brain networks. The authors cover network measures, their interpretation in neuroscience contexts, and applications to understanding brain organization in health and disease.

## Significance

The definitive review of graph theory in neuroscience. Cited thousands of times, this paper established the vocabulary and methods for brain network analysis used throughout the field.
