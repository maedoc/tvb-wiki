## Summary
Systematic analysis of sparsely connected spiking networks. Characterizes different dynamical regimes (synchronous, asynchronous, oscillatory) and their dependence on synaptic parameters.

## Key Concepts
- Sparse connectivity effects
- Asynchronous irregular (AI) states
- Synchronous regular (SR) states
- Fast oscillations from inhibition
- Mean field theory predictions
