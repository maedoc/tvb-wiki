## Summary
Second volume of comprehensive theoretical neurobiology text. Covers nonlinear and stochastic theories including cable theory, stochastic processes, and population dynamics.

## Key Concepts
- Stochastic cable theory
- Random walk models
- Diffusion approximations
- Population density methods
- First passage time problems

## Relevance to TVB
- Stochastic methods for [[neural mass model]]s
- Population-level descriptions
- Foundation for [[stochastic differential equations]]
