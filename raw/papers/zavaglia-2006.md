# Comparison of a mean-field model of electroencephalographic activity to individual brain networks

**Authors**: Lucia Zavaglia, Laura Astolfi, Federico Babiloni, Melani B.C.  
**Published**: 2006  
**Journal**: IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology  

## Summary

Application of neural mass modeling to individual subject brain networks, demonstrating how subject-specific structural connectivity can be combined with population dynamics to generate personalized EEG predictions.

## Key Contributions

- Individualized neural mass model implementation
- Integration of empirical structural connectivity matrices
- Comparison across subjects showing variability in simulated EEG
- Framework for personalized brain modeling using mean-field approaches

## Method

Coupled Jansen-Rit models at each network node, connected via empirical DTI-derived structural connectivity, showing how individual anatomical differences produce different EEG spectral profiles.
