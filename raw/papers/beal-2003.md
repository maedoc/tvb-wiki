## Summary
Comprehensive treatment of variational inference methods for approximate Bayesian inference. Covers mean-field approximations, message passing, and applications to probabilistic models.

## Key Concepts
- Mean-field variational inference
- Factorized approximations
- Message passing algorithms
- Evidence lower bound (ELBO)
- Model selection criteria

## Relevance to TVB
- Foundation for [[variational bayes]] in DCM
- Approximate inference for complex models
- Links to [[free energy principle]]
