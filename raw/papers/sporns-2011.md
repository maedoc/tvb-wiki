# Networks of the Brain

**Author:** Olaf Sporns  
**Year:** 2011  
**Publisher:** MIT Press

## Key Contributions

- Comprehensive textbook on brain network organization
- Integration of network science concepts with neuroscience
- Treatment of structural and functional connectivity
- Discussion of network development, disease, and dynamics

## Summary

This book provides a comprehensive introduction to the field of brain network analysis. Sporns covers the fundamental concepts of network science and their application to understanding brain organization. The book discusses how network approaches can reveal principles of brain architecture, how networks develop and change, and how they are altered in disease.

## Significance

The definitive textbook on brain networks and connectomics. Essential reading for anyone entering the field. Establishes the theoretical framework for understanding brain connectivity using graph theory and network science.
