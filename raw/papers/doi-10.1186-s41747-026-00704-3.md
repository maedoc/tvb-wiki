## Abstract

Population-based arterial input function reduces inter-observer variability and sensitivity to aortic region-of-interest placement artifacts. Whole-kidney modeling avoids complex segmentation of the cortex and medulla regions. DCE-MRI using population-AIF and whole-kidney modeling yields eGFR and RPF significantly correlated with serum and ASL references.
