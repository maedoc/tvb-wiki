# Electric Fields of the Brain: The Neurophysics of EEG (2nd Edition)

**Authors:** Paul L Nunez, Ramesh Srinivasan  
**Year:** 2006 (2nd ed)  
**Venue:** Oxford University Press

## Summary
Comprehensive treatment of the physics underlying EEG signal generation, volume conduction, and source localization. Bridges neuroscience and physics perspectives on scalp-recorded potentials.

## Key Contributions
- Volume conduction theory
- Source localization methods
- Neural mass models of EEG generation
- Connection between microscopic and macroscopic neural activity

## Significance for Whole-Brain Modeling
Critical for understanding how neural mass model outputs relate to measurable EEG signals. Volume conduction equations link model variables to predicted scalp potentials.
