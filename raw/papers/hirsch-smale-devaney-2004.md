## Summary
Comprehensive graduate text covering ordinary differential equations, dynamical systems theory, and chaos. Includes linear systems, phase portraits, stability theory, and chaotic dynamics with applications to science and engineering.

## Key Concepts
- Existence and uniqueness theorems
- Linear systems and matrix exponentials
- Stability analysis (Lyapunov)
- Phase portraits and flows
- Chaotic attractors and symbolic dynamics

## Relevance to TVB
- Mathematical foundation for [[neural mass model]] equations
- Stability analysis for [[whole brain]] simulations
- Phase space methods for [[brain oscillations]]
