## Summary
Introduces normalizing flows for flexible variational inference. Enables rich posterior approximations through invertible transformations, improving inference in complex probabilistic models.

## Key Concepts
- Normalizing flows
- Invertible neural networks
- Change of variables formula
- Amortized inference
- Flexible posterior approximations

## Relevance to TVB

- Flexible inference for complex [[neural mass model]]s
- Modern alternative to Laplace approximation
