# Functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (2nd Edition)

**Authors:** Scott A Huettel, Allen W Song, Gregory McCarthy  
**Year:** 2009 (2nd ed)  
**Venue:** Sinauer Associates

## Summary
Comprehensive textbook covering fMRI principles, acquisition, analysis, and applications. Standard reference for understanding the technical and methodological foundations of functional neuroimaging.

## Key Contributions
- Physics of MRI signal generation
- Experimental design for fMRI studies
- Preprocessing and statistical analysis pipelines
- Applications in cognitive neuroscience

## Significance for Whole-Brain Modeling
Essential reference for understanding fMRI data characteristics, noise sources, and analysis methods that models must account for or replicate.
