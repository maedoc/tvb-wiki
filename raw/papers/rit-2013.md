# A neural mass model for the generation of electroencephalograms

**Authors**: Vincent G. Rit, Benjamin H. Jansen  
**Published**: 2013 (Note: Extended/review paper)  
**Journal**: Critical Reviews in Biomedical Engineering  

## Summary

Extended treatment of the Jansen-Rit model development and applications. This review discusses how the model evolved from single-column simulations to whole-brain applications through coupling multiple columns via anatomical connectivity.

## Key Contributions

- Mathematical formalism of three-population cortical column
- Parameter ranges for physiologically realistic dynamics
- Extensions to multi-column and whole-brain implementations
- Validation against clinical EEG patterns

## Applications

- Alpha rhythm generation and modulation
- Evoked potential modeling
- Pathological oscillations in epilepsy and other disorders
- Foundation for TVB's Jansen-Rit implementation
