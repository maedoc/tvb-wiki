# Spontaneous fluctuations in brain activity observed with functional magnetic resonance imaging

**Authors:** Michael D Fox, Marcus E Raichle  
**Year:** 2007  
**Venue:** Nature Reviews Neuroscience 8(9):700-711

## Summary
Comprehensive review of resting-state fMRI and spontaneous brain activity. Discussed the default mode network and other intrinsic connectivity networks, their physiological basis, and clinical applications.

## Key Contributions
- Review of resting-state fMRI phenomenon
- Default mode network characterization
- Discussion of spontaneous activity significance

## Significance for Whole-Brain Modeling
Essential background for understanding what resting-state models should reproduce. Discusses the organization of spontaneous activity into coherent networks that models must capture.
